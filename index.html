FIND THE SECRET WORD

How to Play

🎯 Objective: Locate and flag all unique letters that make up the hidden 5-letter secret word.

⛏️ Dig Mode: Left-click tiles to uncover them safely. Uncovered safe tiles turn grey or yellow.

🟨 Yellow Tiles: The number reveals how many hidden secret word letters touch that specific tile (including diagonals).

🚩 Flag Mode: Right-click anywhere (or click the Mode button to use left-click) to plant flags on secret letters.

💥 Danger: Digging into a letter that belongs to the secret word triggers an immediate explosion and game over.
